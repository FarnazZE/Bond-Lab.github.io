Welcome to BOND lab at University of Oklahoma!

We study the network organization of brain and behavior in
healthy and clinical populations using computational approaches,
such as network modeling and analysis.


Our objectives are three-fold:

Structural Brain Networks

Understand the network organization of anatomical connections in brain networks and their role in shaping brain function.

  • Learn More

Functional Brain Networks

Examine the structure and dynamics of
functional brain networks.

  • Learn More

Brain Network-behavior Relationships

Understand how brain's connectivity patterns support various behaviors and cognitive processes.
